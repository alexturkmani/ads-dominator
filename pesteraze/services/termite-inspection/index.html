Termite Barrier Systems

Chemical termite barriers create a treated zone around your property that either repels or kills termites attempting to enter. Modern barrier systems are safe, odourless, and provide long-lasting protection.

We install leading barrier products including Termidor, Altriset, and Premise, all backed by manufacturer warranties. Contact us to discuss which barrier system is right for your property.

Termite Baiting Systems

Baiting systems are an effective, non-invasive option for termite control. Bait stations are installed around your property and monitored regularly. When termites find the bait, they share it with the colony, eventually eliminating the entire nest.

Our baiting programs include:

  • Strategic station placement
  • Regular monitoring visits
  • Colony elimination
  • Ongoing protection

Signs of Termite Infestation

Early detection is crucial. Learn more about identifying termites in our blog post: 10 Warning Signs of Termites in Your Home.

Common signs include:

  • Mud tubes on walls, foundations, or timber
  • Hollow-sounding wood when tapped
  • Bubbling or cracked paint on timber surfaces
  • Discarded wings near windows or doors
  • Frass (termite droppings) near timber
  • Tight-fitting doors or windows

If you notice any of these signs, contact Pest Eraze immediately for an urgent inspection.

Termite Prevention Tips

While professional protection is essential, there are steps you can take to reduce termite risk:

  1. Fix any moisture problems around your property
  2. Ensure good drainage away from foundations
  3. Remove timber debris and woodpiles from near your home
  4. Maintain gaps between soil and timber structures
  5. Schedule annual termite inspections

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get a termite inspection?

We recommend annual termite inspections for all Sydney properties. Homes in high-risk areas (near bushland, older homes, or those with previous termite activity) should consider inspections every 6 months. Learn more about inspection frequency recommendations.

What are the signs of termite infestation?

Common signs include hollow-sounding wood, mud tubes on walls or foundations, discarded wings, frass (termite droppings), bubbling paint, and doors or windows that stick. Read our complete guide: 10 Warning Signs of Termites.

How much does termite treatment cost in Sydney?

Treatment costs vary based on property size, treatment type, and severity of infestation. Inspections start from $250, while full barrier treatments typically range from $2,000-$5,000. Contact us for a free quote tailored to your property.

Is termite treatment safe for my family and pets?

Modern termiticides are specifically designed to be low-toxicity to humans and pets while being highly effective against termites. We also offer eco-friendly treatment options. Our technicians will explain all safety precautions.

How long does termite treatment last?

Chemical barriers typically last 5-8 years, while baiting systems provide ongoing protection as long as they're maintained. We provide written warranties and recommend regular inspections to ensure continued protection.
